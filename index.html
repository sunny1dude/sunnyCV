An introduction

Hi! I'm Sunny Arya, CAAST fellow, currenly living in the New Delhi, India. Science always keeps me curious.......

I am a Ph.D. Scholar currently doing research in Highthroughput Plant Phenotyping using Hyperpectral sensor at the Indian Agricultural research indtitute, New Delhi. I am also interested in Biological Imaging & machine learning, computer vision, and its application in agriculture.

I am actively participating in conference, presentaion and seminars in the areas of remote sensinig, Big Data, Watershed Prioritization, AI in Plant diseases and digital agriculture.

I have completed my M.Sc. at Indian Agrcicultural Research Institute and currenly pursuing my PhD at the Division of Aghricultural Physics, IARI, New Delhi.

  • M.Sc. in Adricultural Physics, IARI, 2018
  • B.Sc. in Agriculture, Bidhan Chandra Krishi Vishwavidyala, 2016
